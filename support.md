## Common Questions

### Where is my data stored?

PackMate is local-first. Packing lists, preferences, wallet metadata, and wallet Fdocuments are stored on your device.

### Does PackMate require an account?

No. PackMate does not require an account.

### Does PackMate upload my wallet documents?

No. Wallet documents are stored locally in PackMate’s private app storage.

### Why is city weather unavailable?

PackMate uses Open-Meteo for optional city weather near departure. If the weather request fails, PackMate falls back to country-level seasonal guidance.

### Why am I not receiving departure reminders?

Departure reminders are optional. Check that:

- Departure Reminders are enabled in PackMate Settings
- Notifications are allowed for PackMate in iOS Settings
- the trip has a travel date
- the trip still has unpacked items

### How do I delete my data?

You can delete packing lists and wallet documents in the app. You can delete all local app data by uninstalling PackMate.
