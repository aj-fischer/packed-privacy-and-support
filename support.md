## Common Questions

### Where is my data stored?

Packed is local-first. Packing lists, preferences, wallet metadata, and wallet documents are stored on your device.

### Does Packed require an account?

No. Packed does not require an account.

### Does Packed upload my wallet documents?

No. Wallet documents are stored locally in Packed’s private app storage.

### Why is city weather unavailable?

Packed uses Open-Meteo for optional city weather near departure. If the weather request fails, Packed falls back to country-level seasonal guidance.

### Why am I not receiving departure reminders?

Departure reminders are optional. Check that:

- Departure Reminders are enabled in Packed Settings
- Notifications are allowed for Packed in iOS Settings
- the trip has a travel date
- the trip still has unpacked items

### How do I delete my data?

You can delete packing lists and wallet documents in the app. You can delete all local app data by uninstalling Packed.
